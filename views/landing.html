Automated Wellness Evaluation

Your Health Status,
Always Up to Date

FitAlchemy automatically evaluates three pillars of your wellness using real data — no manual entry needed.

🏋️
Physical Status
BMI-Based

Calculated from your height and weight. Displayed on a live gauge — Underweight, Normal, Overweight, or Obese.

⚡
Fitness Status
Activity-Based

Based on your saved workout count and estimated calorie burn compared to WHO weekly activity targets.

🧠
Mental Status
Sentiment-Based

Analysed from your CoachAlchemy chat history — positive interactions raise your score toward "Motivated".
